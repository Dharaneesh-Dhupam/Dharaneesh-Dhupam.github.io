I’m Dharaneesh Dhupam. I studied Electronics and Communication Engineering at Indian Institute Of Information Technology, Kurnool (2020–2024) and also studied Economics, Sociology, and Public Administration (2022–2025). I wanted to understand both how systems are built and how they break.

During college, I saw how simple processes like buying land could turn into long, confusing, and sometimes unfair experiences. That frustration turned into a question: can we build systems that people can actually trust? That question has been the thread through most of what I’ve built since then.

I’m now working on Systems Change, a platform that tries to make property transactions simple, verifiable, and fraud-proof.

Systems Change, Inc.

Own property faster, smarter, safer. You find a property, sign, and we take care of the rest. The idea is to make buying or selling land as straightforward as possible. Also we want to replace the current government admin system with a more transparent and verifiable system.

Live at sysc.land

How it works

  1. Discover and verify properties with transparent records.
  2. Approve with a digital signature. No paperwork.
  3. We handle the filings and send you the final documents.

Why this matters

  • Security with post-quantum digital signatures (ML-DSA).
  • Immutable and decentralized ownership history.
  • Fast and clear process that cuts out confusion.
